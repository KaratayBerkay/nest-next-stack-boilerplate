The Art of Gesture Navigation

📅 Jul 10, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read

Modern mobile interfaces have embraced gesture-based navigation as a primary way to move through apps. The swipe-left-to-go-back pattern has become a universal language across iOS and Android.

Unlike traditional buttons, gestures feel natural and intuitive. They reduce visual clutter while maintaining discoverability through subtle hints and animations.
